About Me

Hi There!

I'm a multi-disciplinary designer with a passion for all things creative. Currently, I work as a Contract Media Designer in the Centre for Teaching, Learning and Innovation at Lethbridge College.

My interests cover a broad base of creative mediums and every day I am looking for new techniques and ways to add to my creative toolbelt. I have found a passion in leadership positions and I often look for opportunities to lead teams and manage projects. The challenges that leadership roles bring are exciting to me.

I will graduate from Lethbridge College in 2021 with a diploma in Multimedia Production. While in school, I maintained a 4.0 GPA and received the 2020 Louise McKinney Scholarship for my exemplary academic performance.

Every day is a new chance to learn and grow as a designer and a person. I am excited to be starting my journey into the depths of the creative industry!
