"Cube" - 3D Animation

I am always learning new creative techniques whenever I get the chance. With this video, I wanted to explore 3D animation, isometric perspective, and adding personality to a plain geometric object. The general flow of the story is that every time the cube comes into the new room, it feels the same urge to get to the next door. Low and behold, the cube always ends up back where it began and must climb the obstacle in its way. The future of this project is to develop multiple rooms, inside each of these rooms there would be a different challenge for the cube to overcome.
