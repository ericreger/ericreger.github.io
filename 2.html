"Alphabet Project" – Vector Art

26 days, 26 designs. This was no easy task! However, this alphabet is easily one of my favourite projects that I have ever completed. For 26 days I created one letter from the alphabet and shared it with followers on my Instagram account. This challenge tested my ability to push through creative burnout and find inspiration in the most curious of places. Some days I had no ideas at all; however, that was the big idea of this entire project… (idea-inception). In terms of technical learning, everyday was a new opportunity for me to learn a skill or technique to make my letters pop. Illustrator is easily one of my favourite programs from the entire creative suite, it has never let me down!
