"Mannequin Love Story" – Raster Art

Personal projects are so important to me as a designer. This piece came into fruition one day when I stumbled upon a few unique mannequin assets. I immediately fired up Photoshop and got right to work on a captivating visual story. My goal was to try and pull a deeper meaning out of their silent expressions. The black rectangle behind them serves as a connector between the pieces, similar to a frame-by-frame film. The first pair represents young love, holding hands on the school playground. Then comes real feelings, a truly deep connection. The third depicts a quiet whisper, "I love you.”
