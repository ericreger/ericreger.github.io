"Red" – Video Editing

In this project, the task was to use green screen footage in a unique way and the rest was up to my discretion. Whenever I get the chance to have full creative freedom, it often leads to some very exciting projects and unique and original creative discoveries. Right away, I knew I wanted to do a horror style video because Halloween was approaching quickly. I played around with a few visual styles, but the grunge aesthetic helped to reinforce the fear in the story. The project was a great success, and I learned a tremendous amount about green screen overlays. I can say with certainty that the results were quite spine-chilling! Clown/Horror Trigger Warning!
